Cookies

A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor’s computer, and that the visitor’s browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. appoint uses cookies to help appoint identify and track visitors, their usage of appoint website, and their website access preferences. appoint visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using appoint’s websites, with the drawback that certain features of appoint’s websites may not function properly without the aid of cookies.

This Privacy Policy covers the use of cookies by appoint and does not cover the use of cookies by any advertisers.
